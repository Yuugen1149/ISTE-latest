ABOUT US

Established in 2004, the ISTE Student Chapter at Sree Chithra Thirunal College of Engineering is dedicated to equipping students with the technical know-how, practical application skills, and leadership qualities needed to excel in the modern engineering landscape. We strive to redefine technical education by fostering innovation, collaboration, and excellence. Our chapter has grown to become a vibrant technical community, bringing together students passionate about technology, research, and continuous learning.
